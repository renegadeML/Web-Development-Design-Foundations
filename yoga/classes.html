Yoga Classes

Gentle Hatha Yoga
A 60 minute class of poses and slow movement that focuses on asana (proper alignment and
posture), pranayama (breath work), and guided meditation to foster your mind and body
connection. This class is intended for beginners and anyone wishing a grounded foundation in the
practice of yoga.

relieving stress and fostering a sense of well- being. This calming, restorative experience is suitable
for students of any level of experience.
